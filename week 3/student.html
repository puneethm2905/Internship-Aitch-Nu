STUDENT DROPOUT RISK PREDICTION

FOR HIGHER EDUCATIONAL INSTITUTIONS

Student dropout in higher education poses a significant challenge with broad social and economic consequences. Dropout rates vary widely across countries and institutions, influenced by multiple factors including socioeconomic conditions, academic performance, and institutional changes. Addressing this issue proactively requires accurate prediction models capable of identifying at-risk students early.

This project delivers a comprehensive web-based Student Dropout Prediction system that combines advanced machine learning techniques with a user-friendly Flask web application. Utilizing a diverse set of academic, demographic, and socioeconomic features, trained on a balanced and realistic synthetic dataset reflecting dropout tendencies, our platform predicts the likelihood of student dropout with high accuracy.

The system preprocesses inputs systematically and generates real-time risk assessments along with personalized recommendations aimed at reducing dropout risk. Secure user authentication, persistent database storage, and an interactive dashboard support educators and administrators in implementing timely, targeted interventions.

This integration of predictive analytics with Learning Analytics principles empowers higher education institutions to improve retention, boost student success, and ultimately support socio-economic advancement. Building on recent machine learning advancements, our system employs algorithms such as Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, and Neural Networks to optimize accuracy.

To ensure fairness and reduce bias, the model is trained on a dataset balancing demographic diversity and academic profiles. The interactive Flask interface allows seamless data input, visualization of dropout trends, and easy access to student history. The platform supports multi-user roles, enabling faculty, counsellors, and administrators to collaborate efficiently.

Continuous monitoring and retraining ensure adaptability to new policies and changing student populations. Emphasizing data privacy and compliance with educational standards, detailed analytics and reporting provide actionable insights for evidence-based decision making. By integrating predictive insights with personalized interventions, the system aims not only to predict but also to prevent dropout, fostering resilient higher education ecosystems prioritizing student well-being and achievement.

Through this holistic approach, we envision significantly mitigating educational attrition.

Advanced ML Models
